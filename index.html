About Me

Hi, I’m Katie Chedgzoy! I recently graduated with a first class Masters degree in Mathematics from the Uni of York, and I’m currently training to be a full stack developer with Futureproof.

I was first introduced to coding during my degree, and have been fascinated ever since. In just a few weeks at Futureproof, I’ve learnt how to create full stack web applications, and I can’t wait to learn more.

I’m always looking for something new to learn or a new problem to solve. I’m excited to pursue a career in technology where I can challenge myself this way and further my programming knowledge!

When I’m not busy programming, I love reading, travelling, and playing the drums.
